Research Gap

Current dyslexia intervention systems typically focus on only one learning challenge — reading or spelling — and rarely combine emotional monitoring, physiological stress detection, and motor analytics within a single platform.

Most available applications are static and do not adapt learning content based on learner emotions or real-time behaviour. As a result, learners may lose motivation or experience frustration, reducing the long-term effectiveness of the intervention.

NeuroSpark addresses this gap by integrating multiple technologies into one intelligent adaptive framework — bringing together NLP, emotion AI, biosensing, and motor analytics for the first time in a unified system designed specifically for dyslexic learners.
