Literature Survey

Existing dyslexia support systems mainly focus on reading improvement, spelling correction, handwriting support, or memory exercises in isolation — these solutions are generally single-dimensional.

Research studies show that adaptive educational systems improve learner motivation when tasks are personalised according to learner ability. Emotion-aware learning environments have also demonstrated a positive impact on student engagement.

Recent machine learning advancements — including Natural Language Processing, Convolutional Neural Networks, and wearable biosensors — are creating new opportunities to improve special education support systems, moving beyond rigid, one-size-fits-all tools.
