Domain

Objectives

Main Objective — To develop an integrated intelligent learning platform that adapts in real time to support dyslexic learners across multiple dimensions of need.

Specific Objectives

  • 01 Implement NLP-powered pronunciation assistance for reading and speech support.
  • 02 Develop facial emotion recognition to dynamically adapt learning difficulty and colour schemes.
  • 03 Integrate IoT biosensor data for real-time physiological stress monitoring.
  • 04 Build motor coordination analytics to track and improve handwriting and fine motor skills.
  • 05 Personalise learning sessions dynamically based on combined multimodal signals.
